# EW Navigation App — Product One-Pager

## What the product is

EW Navigation App is a **truck navigation application** designed for professional drivers, providing truck-specific routing, navigation, and compliance-aware guidance.  
It operates as both a **standalone navigation app** and as a **driver-facing execution layer** within the broader **EW Office ecosystem**.

The app serves as the primary interface for drivers during trip execution.

---

## Who it is for

Primary users:
- Professional truck drivers
- Company-employed drivers and owner-operators
- Drivers using personal devices or company-provided tablets

Secondary stakeholders:
- Fleet owners and dispatchers (indirectly, via EW Office)
- Operations and support teams

---

## Core user job-to-be-done

> “Safely and efficiently drive a truck from A to B, following a compliant route, with minimal friction and interruptions.”

Key jobs:
- Navigate truck-safe routes
- Avoid restrictions (height, weight, hazardous goods)
- React to changes during the trip
- Execute assigned transport tasks reliably

---

## Current product positioning

EW Navigation App today is:
- **Freemium** for drivers
- Widely adopted, with a large installed base
- High strategic value, but **low direct monetization**

EW Navigation App is evolving from:
> “Standalone navigation tool”  
to  
> “Execution surface for EW Office transport workflows”

---

## EW Office integration (strategic direction)

EW Office is the **dispatcher / back-office platform** for carriers.

The integration goal is:
- 1:1 alignment between routes planned in EW Office and routes executed in EW Navigation App
- Enable driver–dispatcher workflows (routes, tasks, communication, proof of execution)
- Position Navigation as a **value amplifier** of EW Office, not a standalone utility

---

## Why this product matters strategically

- Large driver reach = leverage point for monetization
- Key entry point into the **Energy & Transport Execution journey**
- Critical enabler for EW Office adoption and stickiness
- Direct impact on customer satisfaction and brand perception
