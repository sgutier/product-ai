# Risks & Constraints — EW Navigation App

This document lists **known risks and constraints** that should be considered during initiative definition, discovery, and prioritization.

Initiatives must explicitly acknowledge relevant risks.

---

## Value risks

- Drivers may not perceive enough incremental value to pay
- Monetization may conflict with expectations of a “free” navigation tool
- Office-driven value may not be visible to drivers

Mitigation examples:
- Early validation with real drivers
- Clear value communication
- Segmented offerings

---

## Usability risks

- Increased complexity from Office integration
- Overloading the driver interface with non-navigation tasks
- Distracting interactions during driving

Mitigation examples:
- Driver-first UX principles
- Minimal interaction while driving
- Progressive disclosure of features

---

## Feasibility risks

- Mobile performance degradation
- Increased crash or ANR rates
- Complex cross-team dependencies slowing delivery

Mitigation examples:
- Guardrail metrics (CFR, ANR)
- Phased rollouts
- Explicit dependency tracking

---

## Viability risks

- App Store and Play Store policy violations
- Legal or contractual limitations (e.g. data usage, pricing)
- Support and operational load not accounted for

Mitigation examples:
- Early Legal and Finance involvement
- Policy review before experiments
- Support readiness checks

---

## Strategic risks

- Fragmentation between Navigation and Office experiences
- Conflicting monetization strategies across products
- Short-term revenue optimizations harming long-term trust

Mitigation examples:
- Clear product narrative
- Portfolio-level review of initiatives
- Guardrails on monetization experiments

---

## Explicit constraints

- Embedded / OEM navigation is out of scope
- Navigation reliability is non-negotiable
- Regulatory compliance varies by geography
- Not all Office features can or should surface in Navigation

---

## Risk handling expectations

Every initiative should:
- Name its **top 1–2 risks**
- State how they will be validated or mitigated
- Define clear stop / rollback criteria
