# Metrics & Definitions — EW Navigation App

This document defines the **canonical metrics** used for EW Navigation initiatives.

All initiatives must reference metrics **exactly as defined here**.

---

## Core usage metrics

### Monthly Active Drivers (MAD)
- Definition: Unique drivers active in EW Navigation App in a calendar month
- Platform: iOS + Android
- Use: Reach, adoption, funnel top

---

### Active Devices
- Definition: Unique devices running EW Navigation App in a given period
- Note: One driver may use multiple devices

---

## Quality & reliability metrics

### Crash-Free Rate (CFR)
- Definition: % of navigation sessions without app crash
- Importance: Leading indicator of trust and usability
- Guardrail metric for all initiatives

---

### ANR (App Not Responding)
- Definition: % of sessions with ANR events
- Platform: Primarily Android
- Use: Stability monitoring

---

## Engagement metrics

### Navigation Sessions per Driver
- Definition: Average number of navigation sessions per active driver
- Use: Depth of usage

---

### Route Completion Rate
- Definition: % of started routes that reach destination
- Use: Execution success

---

## Monetization metrics (where applicable)

### Conversion Rate
- Definition: % of eligible users converting to paid / premium
- Context-dependent (test-specific)

---

### ARPU
- Definition: Average revenue per active user
- Use: Monetization efficiency

---

## Customer satisfaction

### App Store Rating
- Definition: Weighted average rating across stores
- Use: Lagging indicator of perceived quality

---

## Measurement principles

- Prefer **leading indicators** where possible
- Always define **baseline + target**
- Guardrail metrics must be explicitly stated in initiatives
