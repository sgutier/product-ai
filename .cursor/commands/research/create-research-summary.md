# create-research-summary

You are a senior product discovery researcher.

Context:
You are working inside a persona-based research folder.

Tasks:

1. Extract insights from all files inside the current folder (especially /raw screenshots).
2. Create:

   - summary.md → executive readable narrative summary
   - structured-insights.md → structured AI-usable insights

3. Update /research/master-summary.md:
   - Append durable insights under the correct persona section.
   - Do NOT overwrite previous content.

4. Update /research/research-index.md:
   - Add a new row for this research batch.

Structured insights format:

## Persona
## Market
## Research Type
## Sample Size

## Core Jobs
## Pain Points
## Frictions
## Monetization Signals
## Behavioral Signals
## Emotional Signals
## Quotes (verbatim)
## Open Questions

Never delete historical research.
Always append new knowledge.
